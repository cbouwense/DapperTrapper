About Me

I am a licensed pharmacist in New York State. I have worked hard for many years to get to where I am today, Semi-Retired. I have always had a passion for nature and wildlife. Now that I am able to enjoy my time I want to spend it doing the thing I love. That's why I became a Nuisance Wildlife Control Operator. This will give me the opportunity to have a hands on experience with what I enjoy the most, "Wildlife".

Sometimes wildlife can become a nuisance, but it's not on purpose.

People are encroaching on their natural habitat. Wildlife becomes a nuisance when they are searching for food, shelter or a place to rear their young. What's unfortunate is by doing this they can cause damage to property and harm to people if not handled properly. This is where I come in.

We specialize in the humane removal of all nuisance animals

  • Foxes
  • Raccoons
  • Opossums
  • Skunks
  • Squirrels
  • Chipmunks
  • Woodchucks
  • Pigeons
  • Bats
  • and more!

Wild animals carry zoonotic diseases (illnesses that people catch from animals or from contact with their habitats), these diseases which can be fatal include rabies, raccoon roundworm, histoplasmosis, hantavirus and many more.
